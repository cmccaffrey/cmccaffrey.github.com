Voltage Coffee & Art is a coffeehouse and gallery located in the buzzing hive of Kendall Square. Serving simple food alongside the finest coffee, tea and chocolate, we hope to satisfy the palate as well as stimulate creative thought. Drop by to grab a really good cup o’ joe (or espresso) and to take in some great local art.
