Misquamicut (mis-kwam-ih-cut) is one of the state’s most popular beach destinations in Rhode Island, with over a half mile of beach front. Located just outside of Westerly, the beach and the surrounding area offers many family-friendly activites. Make Misquamicut your summer beach destination, whether it’s your first time visiting or you’ve been coming for years.
