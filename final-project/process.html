Process

Working with me is really easy. First, email me or fill out a short form to discuss your project. The more details you provide, the better (think cake vs. cupcake, colors, theme, guest count, etc.) Generally, I suggest orders are placed 2 weeks in advance — more time is always better.


I'll get in touch with you (phone or email, your preference) and we can discuss your event. Whether it's a birthday party, wedding shower, or corporate party, we'll discuss any ideas you have and collaborate on a solution.
