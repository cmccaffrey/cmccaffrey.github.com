About Me

Baking has been a passion of mine since I was a little girl. I’ve always enjoyed testing out new recipes and even creating some of my own.


My love of baking inspired me to learn about decorating techniques and cake design. I took a few decorating classes to get me started and then began to play around with tips and fondant and colors and all sorts of stuff at home. Friends and family began asking me to make cakes and cupcakes for birthdays, anniversaries, and baby showers and, eventually, my little hobby turned into a full-blown business!


At Lightly Floured, my goal is to create custom-made cakes and cupcakes that not only look great, but taste great as well. Whether it’s matching the style of an invitation or the theme of a party, I work closely with you to ensure the design, colors, and flavor reflect your vision.
